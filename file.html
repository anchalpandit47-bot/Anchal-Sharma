About

Dr. Anchal Sharma is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Psychology at Invertis University, Bareilly. Her academic interests span clinical psychology, applied psychology, social psychology, mental health awareness, and interdisciplinary research. She is experienced in teaching, counseling, psychological assessment, research methodology, SPSS, academic writing, and student mentorship.
